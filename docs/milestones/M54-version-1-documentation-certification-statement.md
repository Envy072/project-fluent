# Overview

This document certifies, internally, that the completed Version 1 documentation package conforms to the governance model it itself established. Where M51 declared the documentation programme finished, this document specifically attests that the finished package actually satisfies the standards it set for itself — that it practices what it prescribes. It performs no new verification of its own; it certifies based entirely on the evidence M47 and M48 already produced, and is not a legal certification, an external audit, or a certification of the product itself.

---

# Certification Philosophy

- **Internal Certification:** This is a self-certification against the project's own established standards — never an external, legal, or third-party certification.
- **Governance Conformance:** This certification confirms the documentation package actually follows the Change Lifecycle, Traceability requirements, and Constraints it itself defines, per M42 and M46 — that the documentation practices what it prescribes.
- **Documentation Integrity:** Restating M47, the package remains unaltered and internally accurate.
- **Traceability:** Restating M23, every certified claim traces to specific, already-produced evidence.
- **Consistency:** Restating M47, no contradiction exists anywhere across the certified package.
- **Controlled Scope:** This certification is scoped exactly to documentation and nothing more, per Scope Limitations below.
- **Evidence-Based Confidence:** Restating M42 and M44, this certification is not a fresh assertion — it rests entirely on specific, already-produced evidence, per M47 and M48, not an independent judgment invented here.

# Certification Statement

The Version 1 documentation package, M01 through M53, is hereby certified as conforming to the documentation governance it itself established throughout the programme — specifically, M23's Traceability requirements, M33's Documentation Standards, M42's Change Lifecycle and Governance Constraints, and M46's Enterprise Risk and Compliance framework.

This certification rests entirely on the evidence already produced by M47's audit and M48's readiness assessment, reinforced by M49's baseline freeze, M50's handover readiness, M51's completion declaration, M52's archival integrity, and M53's authoritative status. No new verification activity is performed by this document; it certifies on the basis of what those milestones already established.

This certification applies only to the documentation package itself. It is not, and must never be treated as, a certification of any implementation, deployment, or commercial outcome.

# Certification Basis

- **M47** provides the evidentiary finding that the package is internally consistent and free of contradiction.
- **M48** provides the evidentiary judgment that the package satisfies Documentation Readiness across ten distinct domains.
- **M49** provides the fixed, named Baseline against which "the package" being certified is unambiguously defined.

- **M52** provides confirmation that the package's integrity is preserved permanently, meaning this certification's basis does not erode over time.

Together, M47 through M53 constitute the complete evidentiary chain this certification relies upon; none of it is redefined or reproduced here, only cited as the basis for this attestation.

---

# Scope Limitations

**Certified:**
- **Documentation** — that M01 through M53 is complete, internally consistent, traceable, governed, and stable.

**Not Certified:**
- **Implementation** — whether any part of the Implementation Architecture, per M31, has actually been built.
- **Deployment** — whether the product has been deployed to any environment, per M38.
- **Commercial Readiness** — whether the product is ready to be marketed, sold, or offered to any Learner.
- **Operational Performance** — whether the product, once running, actually satisfies M15's Quality Attributes or M36's Operational Readiness in practice; these require their own future, evidence-based confirmation once something exists to observe.

This certification says nothing whatsoever about any of the four "Not Certified" areas above, and must never be cited in support of any claim about them.

---

# Certification Constraints

1. This certification applies exclusively to the documentation package, M01 through M53, and to nothing else.
2. This certification introduces no external standard, legal certification, or regulatory claim of any kind.
3. This certification rests entirely on evidence already produced by M47 and M48, reinforced by M49 through M53; it performs no new, independent verification of its own.
4. This certification may never be cited as authorization for implementation, deployment, or commercial activity, each of which requires its own separate confirmation, per its own governing milestone.
5. This certification, once issued, is itself subject to the same immutability as every other milestone, per M42.

---

# Certification Glossary

- **Certification:** A formal attestation that a defined package conforms to a specific, already-established standard.
- **Internal Certification:** Certification performed against the project's own governance, without external or legal standing.
- **Certification Basis:** The specific, prior evidence a certification relies upon, rather than reproducing.
- **Scope Limitation:** An explicit boundary clarifying what a certification does and does not attest to.
- **Governance Conformance:** The property of a package actually following the rules it establishes for itself.
